Our Story

Nestled in the hills of Accra, Lebanon Mountain Restaurant was born from a dream — a dream to share the richness of Lebanese heritage through food. Founded by a family with deep roots in the mountains of Lebanon, every dish we serve is crafted with love, tradition, and authenticity.

Our Ambiance

From the soft glow of our lanterns to the subtle scent of spices in the air, Lebanon Mountain is more than just a restaurant — it’s an experience. We’ve created a space that evokes the warmth of a Lebanese home, where friends and families gather to celebrate life over good food.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to bringing the true taste of Lebanon to Ghana — using fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, and heartfelt service. Whether you’re new to Middle Eastern cuisine or a seasoned lover of shawarma, we welcome you with open arms and open hearts.

"In every bite, a story of the mountains. In every visit, a memory made."
